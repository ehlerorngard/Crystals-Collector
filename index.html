Crystals Collector

Each round you will be given a new target number. Your goal is to exactly hit that target number by clicking on the four crystals, each with a hidden value! Hit the value and you win the round and get to keep the full contents of your cart! Go over and you lose them... Each new round brings a new target value and new crystal values.
